About Me

The story behind
the engineer

I started out studying Chemistry Education — which gave me something most engineers lack: the ability to break down the complex and make it genuinely understandable. That instinct now powers everything I do.

Today, I'm a DevOps & Cloud Engineer who has shipped production-grade infrastructure on AWS and Azure, orchestrated Kubernetes clusters, built CI/CD pipelines, and delivered real platforms for real clients — from Dubai's real estate market to Amsterdam's jewelry scene to the African diaspora.

As an instructor at Bloomy Technologies, I teach Linux, Cloud, DevOps, Cybersecurity and Data Analytics. As Managing Director of Swelerion Global Ltd, I consult on cloud strategy and infrastructure for organizations. And as a graphics designer, I've been creating visual work week in, week out — flyers, brand identities, event graphics, social media assets.

I design. I build. I teach. I consult. Not as separate things — as one cohesive approach to solving problems with craft.
